Hello, I'm Nishat!

I am an aspiring software engineer living in Queens, New York. I graduated back in 2019 with a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration for Health Professionals from the Macaulay Honor’s Program at Brooklyn College. During my years in college, I thought my future would be in medicine and focused on developing my skills in the healthcare field. After graduation, I worked as a medical scribe.

Throughout the pandemic, I had the opportunity to reflect upon my various experiences in both healthcare and coding. I decided I wanted to work as a software engineer so, I started the engineering immersion program at Thinkful this past fall.

About Me!

It is nice to use a website that is both functional and beautiful. I have always wondered what makes a useful website. I am excited to learn both the front end and back end of software development. I look forward to collaborating with others to create beautiful projects.

Outside of software engineering, I enjoy drawing and learning languages. I draw digitally consistently and find it is an excellent way to relax. I am conversationally fluent in Bengali and Hindi. I am currently self-learning Korean and hope to be fluent one day.
