Hello, I'm Nishat!

I am an aspiring software engineer living in Queens, New York. I graduated back in 2019 with a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration for Health Professionals from the Macaulay Honor’s Program at Brooklyn College. During my years in college I thought my future would be in medicine and focused on developing my skills in the healthcare field. After graduating I worked as a medical scribe.

Throughout the pandemic I had the opportunity to reflect upon my various experiences in both healthcare and coding. I decided I wanted to work as a software engineer and it has brought me to the engineering immersion program at Thinkful.

About Me!

It’s always nice to use a website that both is functional and beautiful. I have always wondered what efforts are put in to making useful websites. I am excited to learn both the front end and back end of software development. I am looking forward to collaborating with others to make wonderful projects.

Outside of software engineering, I enjoy drawing and learning languages. I draw digitally on a consistent basis and find it is a nice way to relax. I am conversationally fluent in Bengali and Hindi. I am currently self-learning Korean and hope to be fluent one day.
